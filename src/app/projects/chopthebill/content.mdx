ChopTheBill is a progressive web application designed to make sharing expenses a
breeze. Adding friends, joining groups, creating expenses and settling
obligations is just a few clicks away.

## Features

- Friends
- Groups
- Notifications
- Summary of pending and settled obligations
- Multiple currencies support (EUR, GBP, JPY, PLN and USD by default)
- Internationalization (English and Polish by default)
- Dark and light themes
- Vibrant and toned down color palettes
- Progressive Web App

## My role

ChopTheBill was one of many student projects made as a result of close
collaboration between
[The Faculty of Electrical and Computer Engineering](https://weii.prz.edu.pl/en/)
and [The Faculty of Management](https://wz.prz.edu.pl/en/). Due to my prior
experience in creating web applications I was appointed as a project leader of a
team of seven developers, meaning I had many duties, including:

- Communicating with product owner and product manager
- Planning and holding regular team meetings
- Overseeing project progress
- Representing the team in front of lecturers
- Assigning tasks
- Initialising the project
- Making architectural and technological decisions
- Conducting code reviews
- Peer mentoring
- Designing a logo
- Implementing home page and the expense module
- Deploying the application
- Giving a presentation on the results of our work

Despite extensive requirements our team managed to reach the objectives within
about two months of work and the project was considered a success.
